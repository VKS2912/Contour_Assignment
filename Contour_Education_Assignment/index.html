Hey! I’m Jeffrey and I believe the key to success in VCE Chemistry is in two important steps. First is strong foundations, and a thorough, intuitive understanding of Chemistry concepts. And second, once you’ve nailed your understanding, is focused and rigorous practice to master your exam skills!

In my classes, I will help you break down difficult concepts into bite-sized pieces that are easy to remember and recall. We will then apply this knowledge together to challenging practice questions, to further refine your understanding, and ensure you’re best prepared for your SACs as well as the Final Exam.

And we’ll do all of this by finding interest in the way Chemistry works. Before you know it, you’ll be cruising your way through the subject, while enjoying your time at tutor.

Whether you are struggling with Chemistry, or aiming for a 45+ raw score, I’ll make it my goal to provide you with the support you need, and equip you with the exact set of tools you’ll require to tackle VCE Chemistry with confidence!
